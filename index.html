Intro

We’re building a next-generation sports technology company that designs smart, data-driven devices to help athletes of all levels improve their technical performance through real-time feedback, in-depth performance analytics, and highly personalized training insights.

Our mission is to empower athletes with intelligent tools that merge cutting-edge sensor technology and advanced AI software to transform how they train, compete, and evolve—enabling smarter, faster, and more effective skill development.
